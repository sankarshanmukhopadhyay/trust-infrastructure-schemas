## Authority

This repository owns the artifact classes listed in its machine-readable authority record. It does not acquire authority merely by referencing an artifact from another repository.

## Promotion and withdrawal

Artifacts move from experimental to provisional or stable only through documented review, validation, and maintainer approval. Unsafe, misleading, or superseded artifacts may be deprecated or withdrawn with an auditable rationale.
